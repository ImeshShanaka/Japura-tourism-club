ABOUT US

About the Japura Tourism Club

The Japura Tourism Club is the official student organization dedicated to tourism and hospitality at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura (USJ). Established under the guidance of the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences (FHSS), we serve as the vital bridge connecting academic knowledge with the dynamic realities of the global tourism industry..
